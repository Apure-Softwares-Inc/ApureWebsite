01 / Research

UX research + Product strategy

Learn what users need, where they struggle and what will help them take action. We turn research into clear product direction so your team can decide what to simplify, test or build first.

Best for

Startups, founders, product teams, nonprofits and businesses that need insights before redesigning or building.

Good fit if you are asking

  • Why are users dropping off?
  • Do people understand the product?
  • Is this idea worth building?
  • What should we fix first?

What you receive

  • Research plan and interview guide
  • User interviews or usability tests
  • Survey and competitor review
  • User journey and findings report
  • Prioritized recommendations

The useful outcome

A shared, evidence-based understanding of the problem and a clear order for the next decisions.

Apure’ conducted a competitive audit and market opportunity analysis for a grocery delivery concept focused on Toronto’s multicultural communities.
