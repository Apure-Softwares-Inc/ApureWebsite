Case study

Making a Kids’ Award Nomination Process Simple, Fun and Accessible

Apure’ partnered with a startup founder to design an engaging nomination process that helps children participate in the Game Awards.

Industry

Gaming / Events

Services rendered

Product strategy, UX research, UI design, prototyping, usability testing, design system and frontend engineering

Related package

Grant-Ready Prototype

Plan a grant-ready prototype
Challenge

Make forms work for children.

The original nomination process was too complex for children. Forms were difficult to understand, and the experience lacked the playful structure needed to maintain a child’s interest.

Approach

Research with children and parents.

  • Used focus groups to understand where children and parents needed more guidance.
  • Translated the nomination process into simpler screens and clearer labels.
  • Added playful support through Nomii the Dragon and age-appropriate onboarding cues.
  • Refined the prototype through usability testing so the concept could support funding conversations.
Impact

A clearer fundable concept.

The new experience made the nomination process easier, clearer and more engaging for children and parents, creating a stronger story for funders, partners and stakeholders.
