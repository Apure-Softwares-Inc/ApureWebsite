Tech Trends We’re Obsessed With this month



What’s Hot (And What’s Not) in Tech Right Now



Agentic AI: Autonomous Decision-Making

Agentic AI marks a major leap in artificial intelligence, shifting from reactive systems to autonomous agents that proactively manage workflows, predict needs, and even negotiate with minimal human input. According to Gartner, 15% of daily work decisions will be made autonomously by such systems by 2028.


Post-Quantum Cryptography(PQC): Future-Proof Security

As quantum computing advances, traditional encryption becomes vulnerable. Post-quantum cryptography (PQC) is now critical, with companies like Google, IBM, and Microsoft adopting quantum-resistant algorithms. HP has already integrated PQC into firmware, following US National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) 2024 standards.


Spatial Computing Goes Mainstream

Combining augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and mixed reality (MR), spatial computing creates immersive digital-physical environments. Beyond gaming, it’s transforming healthcare, education, and retail. The market is projected to surge from US$110billion in 2023 to US$1.7 trillion by 2033, with enterprise adoption accelerating in 2025.
