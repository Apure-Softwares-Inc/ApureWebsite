Meet the founders

The people doing the work are the people in the room.

We keep the process close and personal. You work directly with us from the first conversation through research, design, and handoff, so your project stays clear, focused, and easy to move forward.

Nkechi Oruche, Ph.D.

Co-founder - UX research, product design and software engineering

Nkechi began in Earth Science, researching, analyzing and teaching the stories held in rocks and fossils. Today, she applies the same curiosity and evidence-first thinking to users, products and software.

She bridges research, design and development so useful insights do not get lost between disciplines, and so teams can move from uncertainty to a clear build direction.

Kingsley Oruche

Co-founder - Product design and user research

Kingsley is a senior product designer and user research specialist with a background in psychology and criminology. He looks for the hidden story behind user behavior, then turns tangled experiences into clear, intuitive interfaces.

His strength is making complexity feel calm: identifying what matters, removing what does not and shaping a product people can understand without a manual.
